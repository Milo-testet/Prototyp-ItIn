  • AR-glasses auto-charging

Surplus Energy Feeding

  • Factory grid integration
  • Micro-grid storage
  • Portable powerbank charging
  • Stationary battery systems

How it Works

A simple three-step process that transforms industrial operations

1

Harvest

Workers wear the ExoGrid™ suit during their shift. Every movement, vibration, and body heat generates energy that's captured and stored in the internal buffer system.

2

Auto-charge

When workers touch or connect their devices (AR-glasses, scanners, tools), they automatically receive power from the suit's energy buffer. No manual charging required.

3

Feed Surplus

Excess energy beyond device charging needs is fed back into the local factory grid or stored in micro-grids for use by other systems and equipment.
